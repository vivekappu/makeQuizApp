Quiz Instructions Timer Quiz duration

At the top right corner of your quiz page you will see the timer if the quiz creater has added time duration of quiz, In case if no time is added you won't see timer and you can take as much time as you want. If the quiz has timer then you must finish the quiz before the expires as your quiz get's automatically submitted when timer expires. The timer is in (hours:minutes:seconds) format .

Quiz buttons save button and submit button

At the bottom of your quiz you will see two buttons 1.submit button and 2.save button.
Submit button: Submit button should be used when you have finished your quiz
Save button:In case to avoid accidental refreshing of page to save your responses so that you don't have to answer again it is advised to press save so that your responses till that point is saved and even if browser refreshes you can start from last saved point.

Quiz points where you will see points of the quiz

At the top left corner you will see the total points of the quiz
At the right side corner of each question you will see points for that question

 Other tips How to make use of other features provided

At the bottom of your quiz you will see these radio buttons .
It has three color's, 1.red 2.green 3.blue
red indicates that you haven't answered that question
green indicates that you have answered
blue indicates the question you are currently on.
